I am an architect and designer from NY, currently studying at the Architectural Institute in Prague.
In my free time I am an open source enthusiast, who dreams of free open design and local distributed manufacturing, with an equitable business model!
I help lead Design Disco, a non-profit organization teaching design and design thinking to the youth. I belive everyone can be a designer because design is a problem solving strategy which sees no bordres between professions and disciplins.
